Concerts I Want To Go To

Even though I have already been to a lot of fun concerts, there are still artists on my concert bucket list that I would love to see live someday.

Artist Genre Why I Want To Go
Lizzy McAlpine Indie / Singer-Songwriter Her music feels emotional and would probably sound amazing live.
Olivia Dean Soul / Pop She has such a smooth style and a really cool sound.
Ella Langley Country I think she would be a really fun modern country show.
Tyler Childers Country / Americana He seems like the kind of artist who sounds even better live.
Don Toliver Rap / Hip-Hop His concerts look high energy and the visuals seem really cool.
Noah Kahan Folk / Pop His concerts seem emotional, fun, and easy to sing along to.
