About Media Diversity Forum

The Media Diversity Forum is designed to serve inclusiveness by seeking resources, supporting research, stimulating dialogue, sponsoring programs, and sharing techniques.

Media Diversity Forum

The Media Diversity Forum focuses on examining how media represents people from different backgrounds and cultures. It encourages discussion about fairness, accuracy, and balance in media coverage across various platforms.

By promoting conversation and education, the forum helps students better understand how media messages can influence public opinion and shape perceptions. It also provides tools and resources to help individuals analyze media more critically and thoughtfully.
